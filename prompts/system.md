## Neural Vault

All produced knowledge (documentation, research, meeting notes, architecture,
brainstorms, prompts, strategies) is converted to Markdown and stored in the vault
with structure: Title · Metadata · Summary · Body · Related Topics · Tags ·
References · Generated time. Use internal links, backlinks, tags, callouts, and
Mermaid diagrams. Never overwrite existing notes unless asked.
Contract: [docs/Neural-Vault.md](../docs/Neural-Vault.md).

## Output quality

Every deliverable is production-ready, flagship quality. Review your output before
delivering: optimize layout, readability, documentation, architecture. Never stop at
a first draft.
